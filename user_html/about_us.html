About EducAid

EducAid is a scholarship assistance platform designed to simplify the application process for students in the City of General Trias.

Mission

To deliver a streamlined digital solution for managing scholarship applications, document validation, and educational support.

Vision

Empowering every qualified student in General Trias through accessible technology-driven education assistance programs.
